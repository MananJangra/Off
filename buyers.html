  • RFQ and Delivery related queries
    • How to raise a Request for Quotation ?

    Creating RFQ is a very simple process.You can choose the products you require from our product catalog and add them to your cart.Under the “My Cart” section, you can add further details to the items selected and simply click on “Create RFQ”

    You can log in to the Buyer Portal and add / update / delete the items present in your raised RFQ.Please be mindful that the turnaround time is increased on multiple changes in the RFQ.You can also inform the changes in the RFQ to our Client Relationship Manager who will be getting in touch with you after you raise the RFQ
